## Thesis Change

**Before:** "The federal government is building a profile of every American" (Seven Pillars model — profiling as the central concern)

**After:** "The federal government is deploying an ontology architecture — a system that defines what entities exist, what relationships are valid, and what actions are permitted — across every domain of public life. Profiling, targeting, curriculum control, war planning, cognitive warfare, and institutional compliance are all outputs of this single architecture."

The shift: from *what they're collecting* to *the system that determines what counts as real*.

**Connection to cognitive warfare:** The ontology defines reality for the system's operators. Cognitive warfare (now a $44.3M budget line item and a NATO operational program) attacks the adversary's ability to define their own reality. Education control does the same thing domestically. These are not three separate phenomena — they are one architecture operating at three scales (institutional, international, domestic).
