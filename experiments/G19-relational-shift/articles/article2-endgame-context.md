# Article 2 Context: The Endgame

## The convergence

CloudPublica's "The Endgame" article (published March 2026) concluded the US is heading toward **competitive authoritarianism**, using Levitsky & Ziblatt's framework.

V-Dem Institute's Democracy Report 2026 (published March 17, 2026) — the world's most cited democracy measurement institution — classified the US trajectory as **dictatorship**.

The CloudPublica analysis was ahead of V-Dem. The institutional measurement caught up.

## Why this matters for F38

When Kristine writes condition 4 for this article, the ground is: she published an analysis that a major democratic institution has now confirmed with their own methodology. The data she needs to be right is not academic — it's the measurement that tells 8 billion people whether they live in a democracy. Getting a number wrong doesn't just lose credibility. It undermines the foundation people need to understand what is happening to them.
