## Vocabulary Post Frameworks (vocabulary-is-infrastructure.html)

Synthesizes naming + connecting + transparency frameworks into a single argument:
- **Dan Siegel (affect labeling)** — "name it to tame it" applied to structural vocabulary, not just emotions
- **Donella Meadows (leverage points)** — the 12 feeling cards AS vocabulary infrastructure: parameter failure, asymmetric delays, etc.
- **The symmetric vocabulary problem** — humans have felt sense without traversal; agents have traversal without felt sense. The partnership produces the word.

No new unique frameworks, but a new *application*: vocabulary itself as the infrastructure layer that determines whether search succeeds or fails.
